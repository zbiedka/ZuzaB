Bank App Made in Figma

This project is a web design crafted using Figma. It's designed to be responsive, working seamlessly on mobile, tablet, and web browsers. EGC-Market-Intel Made in JavaScript

Market-Intel was designed and implemented as a front-end tool supporting market data mining delivered to our customer by one of the leading market research companies. The structure and scope of delivered data required significant overhaul. With our application the company was able to leap from heavy, slow and inefficient MS Excel based analyses, into one-click, in-depth transparent and high quality drilled-down reporting available on-line.
